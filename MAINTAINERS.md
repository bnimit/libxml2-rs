## Becoming a Maintainer

Maintainers are contributors who have demonstrated sustained, high-quality contributions over time. The path:

1. Contribute meaningfully to one or more crates (code, tests, or documentation)
2. Participate constructively in design discussions and PR reviews
3. Be nominated by an existing maintainer
4. Be approved by the project lead

Maintainers are listed here with their crate ownership reflected in `CODEOWNERS`.
